# Medical-Insurance-Analysis
Medical Insurance analysis using, Excel, PowerBI, Power Query and DAX

---

## 📌 Overview

The Medical Insurance Cost Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze healthcare insurance costs and patient demographics. The dashboard provides valuable insights into the factors influencing medical charges, enabling users to identify cost patterns and make data-driven decisions.

Using interactive visualizations and filters, users can explore how BMI, smoking habits, age, gender, and geographic region impact medical expenses across different patient groups.

---


## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall medical insurance costs.
- Identify the major factors influencing medical charges.
- Compare medical costs across different BMI categories.
- Examine the impact of smoking on healthcare expenses.
- Explore patient distribution by gender, age group, and region.
- Provide actionable insights through interactive dashboards.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards
- Total Patients
- Total Medical Cost
- Average Medical Charge
- Children Covered

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Microsoft Excel (Data Preparation)

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Smokers incur significantly higher medical charges than non-smokers.
- Medical costs generally increase with age.
- Obese and severely obese patients tend to have higher average medical charges.
- Medical costs vary across different geographic regions.
- Gender distribution among patients is relatively balanced.

---

## 📌 Business Recommendations
- Implement Targeted Smoking Cessation Programs
- Promote Preventive Health and Weight Management
- Prioritize Preventive Care for Older Adults
- Allocate Healthcare Resources Based on Regional Costs
- Continuously Monitor Healthcare Cost Trends
